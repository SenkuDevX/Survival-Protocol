

# Survival Protocol VR
### *Disaster Safety Training - Interactive Simulation*

**Survival Protocol VR** is an immersive, browser-based survival simulation designed to train users on proper safety protocols during life-threatening disasters. Originally developed for a **Safety & Preparedness Workshop**, this project utilizes Three.js to provide a high-fidelity, VR-inspired experience that emphasizes "Drop, Cover, and Hold On" and fire evacuation techniques.

---

## 🌟 Key Features
* **Multiple Scenarios:** Choose from a Bedroom, Living Room, Office (Earthquake training), or a Warehouse (Fire Disaster).
* **Real-time HUD:** A specialized heads-up display tracking your health, score, and active objectives.
* **Dynamic Physics & Hazards:** Experience falling debris, shattering windows, and spreading fire that reacts to your proximity.
* **Interactive Objectives:** * **Take Cover:** Find and occupy designated safe zones.
    * **Equipment:** Locate and equip safety helmets or respirators.
    * **First Aid:** Recover health using hidden medical kits.
    * **Evacuation:** Navigate to the emergency exit once the "All Clear" is given.

---

## 🎮 Controls
To maintain the feel of a VR environment, the simulation uses **Pointer Lock** controls for 360-degree awareness.

| Action | Control |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Move** | `W` `A` `S` `D` or Arrow Keys |
| **Look Around** | Mouse Movement (Click screen to lock) |
| **Interact** | Left Click (Aim crosshair at glowing objects) |
| **Restart** | `ESC` (to unlock mouse) or Button on Fail/Win screen |

## 📋 Workshop Learning Objectives
1.  **Immediate Reaction:** Identifying the "Safe Zone" within 8 seconds of the initial tremor or alarm.
2.  **Hazard Recognition:** Learning to stay away from glass windows and heavy unanchored furniture (Bookshelves/TVs).
3.  **Resource Management:** Understanding that equipment (Helmets/Masks) significantly reduces damage taken during the disaster phase.
